Meteorite
Weather

The developers behind this website were Tyler Friedberg, Charlotte KT, and Ethan Librie. We developed this project to show the different weather in different areas around the world through location tracking. We also added a way to find out meteorites in your area from the last 200 years. We hope you can use this website to get quick weather reports and find out a little history about the area you are in or your own area pertaining to meteorites.
